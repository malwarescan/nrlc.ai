## NOTES

- Primary design system uses: `#12355e`, `#000000`, `#2a4a6e`, `#ffffff`
- Most backgrounds are white (`#ffffff`) or light grays (`#f5f5f5`, `#e8e8e8`)
- Text is primarily black (`#000000`) or muted gray (`#666`, `#2a4a6e`)
- Blue accents vary: `#0066cc` (focus/standard), `#4a90e2` (homepage), `#000080` (course)
- Yellow/amber (`#fff3cd`, `#ffc107`, `#856404`) used only for Prechunking course highlights
