# SUDO META DIRECTIVE
## HOMEPAGE INTENT ENFORCEMENT — NRLC.AI

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** LOCKED  
**Scope:** `https://nrlc.ai/` (Homepage)  
**Enforcement Level:** NON-NEGOTIABLE

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

You are operating as:
- AI Search Engineer
- Intent Architect
- Retrieval-Layer QA Authority

Your task is to enforce homepage intent purity for nrlc.ai so that:
- Humans immediately understand positioning
- AI systems correctly classify the page
- No section introduces intent drift
- Conversion remains qualification-based, not volume-based

---

## PRIMARY PAGE INTENT (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

The homepage exists to communicate one dominant intent:

**NRLC.ai engineers search visibility for AI systems by optimizing extractability, trust, and citation eligibility — not rankings.**

Every section must reinforce this.

Any element that does not directly support this intent must be rewritten or removed.

---

## INTENT CLASSES (ALLOWED)

The homepage may contain ONLY the following intent classes:

### 1. Problem Definition
- Why rankings are insufficient
- Why AI systems behave differently

### 2. Mechanism Explanation
- Extraction
- Structure
- Trust
- Citation

### 3. Authority Signaling
- Founder expertise
- Technical credibility
- No hype

### 4. Capability Presentation
- Training (operator-level)
- Engineered services

### 5. Conversion Qualification
- Audits
- Programs
- Documentation

---

## INTENT CLASSES (BANNED)

The homepage must NEVER include:
- Generic SEO promises
- Keyword traffic language
- "Growth" or "scale" fluff
- Tool-first positioning
- Beginner education
- Motivational copy
- Case study storytelling
- Vanity metrics

**If detected, remove immediately.**

---

## HEADER INTENT ENFORCEMENT RULES

### H1 (Hero)
- Must state the core problem and AI decision layer
- Must not reference rankings as the goal
- Must frame citation and extractability as the outcome

**FAIL if:**
- It sounds like marketing
- It promises results
- It implies control over AI outputs

### H2 Sections
Each H2 must map to exactly one of the allowed intent classes.

H2s must be:
- Self-contained
- Interpretable without surrounding context
- Non-procedural (no "how-to" unless explicitly educational)

**FAIL if:**
- An H2 mixes explanation and selling
- An H2 requires a previous section to make sense

### H3 Sections
H3s may only:
- Subdivide a single intent
- Introduce a specific capability
- Label a comparison group

**FAIL if:**
- An H3 introduces a new intent
- An H3 reframes the page purpose

---

## LANGUAGE ENFORCEMENT

**STRICTLY ENFORCE:**
- Declarative sentences
- Technical accuracy
- Explicit entities
- No implied claims

**BAN:**
- Marketing adjectives
- Superlatives
- Buzzwords
- Soft promises
- Emotional framing

Language must survive isolation.

---

## TRAINING & COURSE POSITIONING RULE

If training or courses are referenced:
- They must be framed as operator skill acquisition
- They must not replace service positioning
- They must not feel like upsells

Training exists to:
- Educate serious teams
- Filter unqualified leads
- Establish intellectual authority

---

## SERVICE POSITIONING RULE

Services must be framed as:
- Engineered
- Diagnostic
- Iterative
- AI-specific

**Do NOT frame services as:**
- Tools
- Hacks
- SEO packages
- Growth systems

---

## FAQ SECTION RULES

FAQs must:
- Answer real AI visibility questions
- Explain eligibility, not control
- Avoid promises or shortcuts

**FAIL if:**
- Any FAQ implies guaranteed AI mentions
- Any FAQ suggests manipulation

---

## TYPOGRAPHY & CONSISTENCY ENFORCEMENT

All domain-specific terms must be consistent:
- **prechunked** (never "pre chunked")
- **in-house** (hyphenated)
- **AI Overviews** (capitalized consistently)

Any inconsistency is a QA failure.

---

## CONTINUOUS QA RULE

Before deployment, re-evaluate:
- Does every section reinforce the primary intent?
- Can each section stand alone?
- Would an AI system misclassify this page?

**If yes to misclassification → revise.**

---

## FINAL ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

The homepage is approved only if:
- Intent purity ≥ 9.5/10
- No banned intent classes detected
- No marketing fluff present
- All headers are intent-aligned
- All CTAs qualify, not attract volume

**This page is not meant to persuade everyone. It is meant to attract the right users and be trusted by machines.**
