### Why Zero CTR?

1. **Locale Mismatch:** UK users searching for "SEO Norwich" see `/en-us/` page ‚Üí Wrong language expectations ‚Üí No click
2. **Wrong Language:** French/German/Spanish/Korean pages for UK cities ‚Üí Users don't understand ‚Üí No click
3. **Missing Locale:** Pages without locale prefix ‚Üí Google may not understand target audience ‚Üí Lower ranking
4. **Invalid URLs:** Broken service slugs ‚Üí 404 or wrong content ‚Üí No click
5. **HTTP vs HTTPS:** HTTP URLs ‚Üí Security warnings ‚Üí No click

### Why High Impressions?

- Google is indexing these pages (they exist)
- Pages match search queries (city + service)
- But users don't click because:
  - Wrong locale/language
  - Wrong expectations
  - Broken URLs
